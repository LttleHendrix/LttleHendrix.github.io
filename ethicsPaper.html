Ethics Paper

This is a paper I wrote for my ethics class. At first glance philosophy and computer science have nothing in common. High level pondering about the nature of the world, versus producing very real and usable products. But in nature, the disciplines share a lot- logic is the core of both. Whether we are deciding how we should live or how we should write a project, the logic must flow seamlessly or the argument will fail or the program will crash. In this paper, I consider the morality of eating meat from factory farms. My argument concludes that it is not something we should do. The practice of logic will inform us in how we should live our lives as well as any computer science project we work on, including Classy Schedule.
